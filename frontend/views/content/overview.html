About Us

A REMARKABLE POSITION AMONG DAIRY INDUSTRY

Vachan, brand of Sarda Dairy & Food Products pvt. ltd. differentiates itself in dairy business model i.e., from dairy farm to finished milk products. Vachan will play a vital role in supplying milk and milk products like curd as milk enriches life with the proteins contained in it.

Sarda Dairy & Food Products pvt. ltd. is devoted to uphold institutional structures that permit milk producers and farmers through processes that are reasonable. A significant portion of its income is ploughed back into the value chain to support and maintain the supply chain system.We are keen to provide a secure and positive work environment with the aim of inspiring pride and ownership in our staffs.

Vision

Service to rural farmers. We are committed to become the pioneering market leader by supplying quality milk and milk products and trustworthy services to our clienteles.

mission

Our mission is to deliver safe and wholesome products consistently to our valued clienteles. To meet this objective we have developed programs and policies focusing on the safety and quality of our products
